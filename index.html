Liz Carpentry

Custom Built Cabnitrey.

No job is too big or too small for Liz Carpentry. Whether dealing with new cabinetry, simple renovations, or high end renovations Miguel has the experience and skills you need. His attention to detail and drive to achieve only the best possible outcome will ensure your total satisfaction. In order to promote excellence, Miguel surrounds himself with only the top craftsmen in the local area. Miguel has worked with the same professionals for a very long time and those partnerships drive consistent high quality results.

It is not unusual for Miguel to have repeat customers, in fact a large majority of his projects come from long term clients. Miguel establishes a one on one client relationship with you during the entire project, from start to finish. He will be able to guide you every step of the way to ensure your 100% satisfaction. Miguel is hands on, he is readily available to talk, and you can be sure that you will be able to reach him throughout the entire project.
